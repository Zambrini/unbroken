# `0.5.0` — Arena Materialized

The approved concepts stage UNBROKEN as a globally watched constructed arena. After threat containment shipped, the live build still read as a flat diagram. This checkpoint materializes the persistent venue without changing the fight beneath it.

## Player-visible result

- Restrained floor sectors, radial seams, and layered shell bevels give the octagonal floor physical depth.
- A built containment rail with corner joints and clamps keeps the arena boundary crisp above hazards.
- Stepped stands and slow deterministic audience lights sell the live-event premise without competing with combat information.
- The cyan relay, magenta prism, camera masts, and red maintenance pod are physically distinct and share one restrained light direction.
- All four fixtures sit between the reachable collider envelope and the visible containment rail, so decorative mass never implies a missing collision.
- Static venue construction is drawn once. Ambient venue lights, hazards, actors, and effects remain dynamic.

The semantic grammar is unchanged: white and cyan identify the challenger and exposed core, dashed magenta queues danger, and solid red marks lethal space.
